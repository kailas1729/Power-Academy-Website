About Power Academy

Welcome to Power Academy, where education meets inspiration!

Power Academy is dedicated to nurturing young minds and shaping futures. With a rich legacy in education, we have been providing quality learning experiences for students of all ages.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in the diverse range of educational services we offer:

  • Playgroup and Kindergarten - Creating a foundation for a lifetime of learning through fun and engaging activities.
  • 1st to 10th (ICSE/CBSE/State Board) - Empowering students with a comprehensive curriculum to excel in their academic journey.
  • 11th and 12th Commerce - Guiding aspiring minds towards success in the field of commerce.
  • 11th and 12th Science - Igniting curiosity and exploration for future scientists and innovators.

At Power Academy, we believe in providing not just education, but an experience that fosters holistic development. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to creating a supportive and inspiring learning environment.

Join us on this educational journey, where every student is a star, and every achievement is a milestone.
